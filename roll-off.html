Roll-off service

Temporary dumpsters for cleanup, construction, and jobsite waste.

Use this page for roll-off requests, swap and pull scheduling, placement notes, and material restrictions. Final pricing depends on container size, haul needs, rental time, location, and tonnage.

What the office needs before scheduling

Requested container size and expected material
Delivery address with gate or access notes
Preferred delivery / pickup window
Whether this is a first drop, pull, swap, or extension

Lead time

Service is often available within 24 hours, but larger jobs and multi-container requests should be called in earlier when possible.

Placement

Keep overhead wires, fences, gates, and soft ground in mind. Bad placement information slows down dispatch and can create failed trips.

Loading rules

Do not load above the top rail. Weight limits, overages, and heavy material loads need review before the final ticket is closed.

Restrictions

Material and loading rules

Restricted materials include hazardous waste, asbestos-containing material, paint, aerosols, liquids, solvents, septic waste, sludge, dead animals, fluorescent bulbs, electronics, tires, batteries, and propane tanks.

Do not overload

Material over the rail can create transport and safety issues and may stop the pickup until corrected.

Keep access clear

Locked gates, blocked drives, or unstable pads create unnecessary failed trips.

Call in changes early

Pulls, swaps, and extended rental time should be requested before the route is finalized.
