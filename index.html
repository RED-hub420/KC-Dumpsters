What this demo is built around

The live business already operates roll-off service, rural pickup, and City of Graham information pages. This version turns that into a cleaner, multi-page product with a real command-center feel.

  • Service request flows for roll-off, rural, and commercial prospects
  • Route updates page for delays, holidays, and customer communications
  • Admin command center for operations, billing, and customer issues
  • Customer portal concept for invoices, upcoming service, and notices
